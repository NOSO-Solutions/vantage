## Tool Record

Each AI tool registered in VANTAGE captures four categories of information:

**Identity** — what the tool is, who makes it, what category it falls into

**Ownership** — who owns it, which department uses it, whether it is Authorized or Unauthorized, and when that status was set

**Data Exposure** — what kind of data the tool touches: customer PII, employee records, financial data, intellectual property, or nothing sensitive

**Risk Signal** — a Low / Medium / High flag derived from authorization status and data exposure, overridable by your team
